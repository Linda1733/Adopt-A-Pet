Boo Bitsy
Boo is a two year old female Pacific Parrotlet. She loves to sing and eat and she requires a little care but a lot of attention. She is good with other pets. Bitsy is a ten-year old Conure. She is very loving bird and her life span is twenty to thirty years. She is a great addition to any family.
Perry Peachy and Perfect
Perry is a six-year old parakeet who loves to sing and is very affectionate. He is not good with other animals and prefers to have a quiet home. Peachy and Perfect are two five-year old Peach Faced Lovebirds. They love to sing and cannot be separated from each other.
